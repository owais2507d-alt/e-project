Meditation Tips for Beginners

Simple guidelines to start your meditation journey

1

Start Small

Begin with just 5 minutes daily. Consistency matters more than duration.

2

Find Quiet Space

Choose a comfortable, distraction-free environment for practice.

3

Focus on Breath

Use your breath as an anchor when your mind wanders.

4

Be Patient

Don't judge your practice. Some days will be easier than others.

5

Use Guided Sessions

Follow along with guided meditations until you feel comfortable.

6

Practice Daily

Make meditation a regular habit, like brushing your teeth.
